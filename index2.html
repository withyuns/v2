Although Korean culture heavily emphasizes on the value of comformity, I’ve always wanted to do something creative, and make a unique contribution to society. Being a fan of Britney Spears, I decided that the best way I could express my support was to develop a website for her. As a result, this piqued my interest in web-design and web-programming. The website I created was recognized as Britney’s most popular website in Korea, so Britney’s advertising agency in Korea proposed that it be the official website of Britney Spears. Through the website I had learn how to interact with users and receive feedback in real time. I also implimented changes in reponse to the users as the web-master. I modified the web as both a developers and a user. I also felt satisfied with learning new technologies at this time
